WHO AM I?

Hey there!

My name is Ricardo Castillo and I'm an international student at The University of Texas at San Antonio.
I was born in Torreon, Coahuila, Mexico where I grew up until I was 19. Currently, I'm getting my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, with a double concentration in Software Engineering and Cyber Security.
I'm very passionate about technology and learning. Similarly, I enjoy designing and innovating when applicable. If given the opportunity, I enjoy teaching people about computers and what you can do with them.
A few of my hobbies include project development, swimming, graphic design, playing drums, coding, traveling, hiking, and outdoors.
I'm a full-time student, but I work part-time at StudentConnect, UTSA, where I learned most of my skills and had hand-to-hand experience with troubleshooting, managing computer labs, and hardware.


On this website you may learn more about me and browse my projects!
